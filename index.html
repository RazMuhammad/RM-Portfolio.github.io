About Me

A brief introduction to my journey and interests.

I am a Computer Science graduate from the University of Agriculture Peshawar. My academic journey has ignited a deep passion for artificial intelligence, leading me to specialize in Machine Learning and Deep Learning. I have dedicated myself to mastering technologies like PyTorch, TensorFlow, and Scikit-Learn, and have a proven track record of developing complex models from the ground up.

My curiosity extends to the cutting edge of AI, with a strong focus on Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs). I have hands-on experience fine-tuning models like Llama-2 and building applications using Hugging Face, LangChain, and various APIs. From creating voice-to-voice chatbots to developing RAG systems for document interaction, I am driven by the challenge of creating practical and innovative AI solutions.
